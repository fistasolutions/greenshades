## Cost Optimization Tips

1. **Start with Free Tiers:** Most tools offer free tiers - use them to evaluate
2. **Annual Billing:** Save 15-20% with annual subscriptions
3. **Team Plans:** Often more cost-effective than individual plans
4. **Prioritize Essential Tools:** Start with core tools for your role
5. **Share Accounts:** For tools that allow it (check terms of service)
6. **Monitor Usage:** Track usage to avoid overpaying for unused features

## Important Notes

1. **Pricing Changes:** All prices are subject to change. Check official websites for current pricing.

2. **Free Tiers:** Many tools offer free tiers with limited features - use these to evaluate before subscribing.

3. **Trial Periods:** Most paid tools offer free trials (typically 7-30 days).

4. **Educational Discounts:** Some tools offer discounts for educational institutions.

5. **Volume Discounts:** Enterprise and large team plans often have better per-user pricing.

6. **Security & Compliance:** Ensure tools meet Greenshades security and compliance requirements before subscribing.

7. **Approval Process:** Follow Greenshades tool approval process (see `00_ai_awareness_and_strategy/04_ai_ethics_and_security_basics/05_approved_ai_tools_list.md`)
