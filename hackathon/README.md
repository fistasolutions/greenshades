### What is This Program?

The Greenshades AI Hackathon Program is a structured innovation initiative that empowers every department to leverage AI and agentic AI technologies to solve real business challenges. The program consists of:

- **Pre-Hackathon Phase:** 8-12 weeks of preparation and skill building
- **Hackathon Phase:** 2-3 days of intensive development and innovation
- **Post-Hackathon Phase:** Evaluation, production development, and scaling
