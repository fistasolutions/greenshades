## Task 3: World-Class Hackathon Guide Template

Each department's `hackathon_guide.md` must include:

### 1. Header Section
- Department name
- Duration
- Date (TBD placeholder)
- Team size and structure
- Target audience

### 2. Hackathon Overview
- **Objective:** Clear, measurable goals
- **Theme:** Compelling, department-specific theme
- **Success Criteria:** Specific, measurable outcomes
- **Expected Impact:** Business value and ROI

### 3. Problem Statements (3-5 problems)
Each problem statement must include:
- **Challenge:** Clear problem description
- **Requirements:** Specific, measurable requirements
- **Expected Impact:** Quantifiable business value
- **Tools to Use:** Specific AI tools and technologies
- **Judging Weight:** Clear evaluation criteria
- **Success Metrics:** How success will be measured
- **Greenshades Context:** How it relates to payroll/tax/employee management

### 4. Hackathon Schedule
- **Day 1:** Kickoff, team formation, development start
- **Day 2:** Development, mentoring, check-ins
- **Day 3:** Final development, presentations, judging, awards

### 5. Team Formation
- Team size recommendations
- Skill mix requirements
- Role assignments
- Collaboration guidelines

### 6. Resources & Tools
- **AI Tools:** GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini
- **Development Tools:** Specific to department
- **Data Sources:** Available datasets and APIs
- **Documentation:** Links to relevant curriculum

### 7. Judging Criteria
- Innovation (30%)
- Technical Quality (30%)
- Business Value (20%)
- Presentation (20%)
- Additional department-specific criteria

### 8. Prizes & Recognition
- First, second, third place prizes
- Special category awards
- Recognition opportunities
- Production deployment opportunities

### 9. Post-Hackathon
- Evaluation process
- Production development roadmap
- Scaling opportunities
- Success stories and case studies

### 10. ESG & 10X Productivity Alignment
- Environmental impact considerations
- Social responsibility aspects
- Governance and compliance
- Productivity improvement metrics
