## Task 2: World-Class Quality Standards

### Hackathon Guide Requirements

Each `hackathon_guide.md` must include:

1. **Header Section**
   - Department name, duration, date, team size, target audience
   - Professional formatting

2. **Hackathon Overview**
   - Clear, measurable objectives
   - Compelling department-specific theme
   - Specific, measurable success criteria
   - Quantifiable business value and ROI

3. **Problem Statements (3-5 problems)**
   Each problem must have:
   - **Challenge:** Clear, specific problem description
   - **Requirements:** Detailed, measurable requirements
   - **Expected Impact:** Quantifiable business value (time savings, cost reduction, productivity gains)
   - **Tools to Use:** Specific AI tools and technologies
   - **Judging Weight:** Clear evaluation criteria breakdown
   - **Success Metrics:** How success will be measured
   - **Greenshades Context:** Specific connection to payroll/tax/employee management
   - **Example Solutions:** Inspiration and guidance

4. **Hackathon Schedule**
   - Detailed day-by-day timeline
   - Specific time slots and activities
   - Mentor check-ins
   - Presentation schedule

5. **Team Formation**
   - Team size recommendations
   - Skill mix requirements
   - Role assignments
   - Collaboration guidelines

6. **Resources & Tools**
   - **AI Tools:** GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini
   - **Development Tools:** Department-specific tools
   - **Data Sources:** Available datasets and APIs
   - **Documentation:** Links to relevant curriculum

7. **Judging Criteria**
   - Innovation (25-30%)
   - Technical Quality (25-35%)
   - Business Value (20-25%)
   - Presentation (15-20%)
   - Department-specific additions

8. **Prizes & Recognition**
   - First, second, third place prizes
   - Special category awards
   - Recognition opportunities
   - Production deployment opportunities

9. **Post-Hackathon**
   - Evaluation process
   - Production development roadmap
   - Scaling opportunities
   - Success stories framework

10. **ESG & 10× Productivity Alignment**
    - Environmental impact considerations
    - Social responsibility aspects
    - Governance and compliance
    - Productivity improvement metrics (quantified)

### Pre-Hackathon Milestones Requirements

Each `pre_hackathon_milestones.md` must include:

1. **Header Section**
   - Department name, hackathon name, duration (8-12 weeks), target audience

2. **Overview**
   - Clear purpose and goals
   - Learning path structure
   - Success criteria

3. **Milestone Structure (8-12 milestones)**
   Each milestone must have:
   - **Milestone Name:** Clear, descriptive name
   - **Duration:** Specific weeks (e.g., Weeks 1-2)
   - **Learning Objectives:** Clear, measurable objectives
   - **Required Curriculum Modules:** Specific lessons with links
   - **Hands-On Exercises:** Practical exercises with checkboxes
   - **Assessment Checkpoint:** Knowledge validation method
   - **Deliverables:** Required outputs
   - **Success Criteria:** How to know you're ready
   - **Time Commitment:** Hours per week

4. **Weekly Breakdown**
   - Week-by-week learning plan
   - Time commitment per week
   - Key milestones per week
   - Check-in points

5. **Tools & Resources**
   - Required tools and setup instructions
   - Learning resources with links
   - Practice projects
   - Community support channels

6. **Assessment & Progress Tracking**
   - Self-assessment quizzes
   - Progress checkpoints
   - Readiness evaluation
   - Support and mentoring

7. **Department-Specific Focus**
   - Customized learning paths
   - Role-specific exercises
   - Real-world Greenshades scenarios
   - Context integration

8. **ESG & 10× Productivity Alignment**
   - Environmental considerations
   - Social responsibility
   - Governance compliance
   - Productivity metrics
