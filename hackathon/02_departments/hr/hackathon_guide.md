## Problem Statements

### Problem 1: AI Resume Screening Agent
**Challenge:** Create an AI agent that automatically screens resumes, matches candidates to job requirements, and ranks candidates.

**Requirements:**
- Screen resumes automatically
- Match candidates to job requirements
- Rank candidates by fit
- Generate candidate summaries
- Support multiple job types

**Expected Impact:**
- 80% reduction in screening time
- 90% accuracy in candidate matching
- Faster hiring process

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (30%), Technical Quality (30%), Business Value (20%), Presentation (20%)

---

### Problem 2: Employee Onboarding Automation
**Challenge:** Build an AI system that automates employee onboarding, generates personalized onboarding plans, and tracks progress.

**Requirements:**
- Automate onboarding workflows
- Generate personalized onboarding plans
- Track onboarding progress
- Generate onboarding reports
- Support multiple roles/departments

**Expected Impact:**
- 70% reduction in onboarding time
- Consistent onboarding experience
- Better employee engagement

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (25%), Technical Quality (35%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)

---

### Problem 3: Employee Engagement Analyzer
**Challenge:** Create an AI agent that analyzes employee feedback, identifies engagement trends, and provides actionable recommendations.

**Requirements:**
- Analyze employee feedback
- Identify engagement trends
- Generate insights and recommendations
- Create engagement reports
- Predict engagement risks

**Expected Impact:**
- 80% reduction in analysis time
- Better engagement insights
- Proactive engagement management

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (20%), Technical Quality (40%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)
