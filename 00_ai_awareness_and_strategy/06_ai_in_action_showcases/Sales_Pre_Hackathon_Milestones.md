# Sales Department: Pre-Hackathon Milestones

**Department:** Sales  
**Hackathon:** AI Lead Qualification & CRM Automation  
**Duration:** 8 Weeks  
**Target Audience:** Sales Managers, Sales Representatives, Account Executives

---

## Overview

This 8-week pre-hackathon program prepares Sales teams for the AI Agentic AI Hackathon by building foundational knowledge in AI-powered lead qualification, proposal generation, and CRM automation.

---
