Design comprehensive milestones that:

Each hackathon should include:
- Clear objectives and success criteria
- Problem statements with real business value
- Team formation guidelines

### 5. World-Class Standards

## Output Requirements

For EACH department, create:

1. **Pre-Hackathon Milestones Document**

   - Hands-on exercises
   - Assessment checkpoints
   - Readiness criteria

2. **Hackathon Guide Document**
   - Hackathon overview and objectives
   - Problem statements (3-5 per department)

- **Completeness:** All departments covered comprehensively

- **Scalability:** Repeatable and adaptable format
