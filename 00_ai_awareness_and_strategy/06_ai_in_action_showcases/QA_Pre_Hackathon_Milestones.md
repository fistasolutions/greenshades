# QA Department: Pre-Hackathon Milestones

**Department:** QA  
**Hackathon:** AI Test Automation & Quality Assurance  
**Duration:** 8 Weeks  
**Target Audience:** QA Engineers, Test Automation Engineers, QA Managers

---

## Overview

This 8-week pre-hackathon program prepares QA teams for the AI Agentic AI Hackathon by building foundational knowledge in AI-powered test generation, automation, and quality assurance.

---

### Learning Objectives
- Understand AI and agentic AI concepts

- Understand test automation with AI
- Complete foundational training

- ✅ `00_introduction_to_ai_and_agentic_ai/` (all 6 lessons)

- ✅ `03_mcp_and_specdriven_development/00_what_is_mcp.md`

**Exercise 1: AI Test Tool Setup**
- [ ] Explore AI test generation tools

- [ ] Configure AI testing environments
- [ ] Test basic AI test generation

**Exercise 2: First AI-Generated Tests**

**Exercise 3: Understanding Test Automation**

### Assessment Checkpoint
