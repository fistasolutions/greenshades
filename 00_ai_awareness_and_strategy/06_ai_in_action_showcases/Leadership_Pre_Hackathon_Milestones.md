# Leadership Department: Pre-Hackathon Milestones

**Department:** Leadership  
**Hackathon:** AI Strategic Planning & ROI Analysis  
**Duration:** 6 Weeks  
**Target Audience:** Executives, Directors, Senior Managers

---

## Overview

This 6-week pre-hackathon program prepares Leadership teams for the AI Agentic AI Hackathon by building foundational knowledge in AI strategy, ROI analysis, and organizational AI governance.

---
