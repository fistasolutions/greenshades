# Leadership Department: AI Strategic Planning Hackathon

**Department:** Leadership  
**Duration:** 1 Day  
**Teams:** 2-3 teams (2-3 people each)

---

## Hackathon Overview

### Objective
Build innovative AI strategic plans that drive organizational AI adoption, maximize ROI, and establish effective AI governance.

### Theme
"AI Strategic Excellence: Strategy, ROI, and Governance"

---

## Problem Statements

### Problem 1: AI Strategic Roadmap Generator
**Challenge:** Create an AI-powered system that generates comprehensive AI strategic roadmaps, including investment priorities, timelines, and success metrics.

**Requirements:**
- Generate AI strategic roadmaps
- Prioritize AI initiatives
- Create investment timelines
- Define success metrics
- Support multiple departments

**Expected Impact:**
- 70% faster strategic planning
- Better AI investment decisions
- Clear AI adoption path

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (30%), Strategic Quality (30%), Business Value (20%), Presentation (20%)

---

### Problem 2: AI ROI Analysis and Business Case Generator
**Challenge:** Build an AI system that analyzes AI investment opportunities, calculates ROI, and generates executive business cases.

**Requirements:**
- Analyze AI investment opportunities
- Calculate ROI for multiple scenarios
- Generate executive business cases
- Prioritize investments
- Support decision-making

**Expected Impact:**
- 80% reduction in analysis time
- Accurate ROI projections
- Better investment decisions

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (25%), Strategic Quality (35%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)

---

## Judging Criteria

1. **Innovation (25 points):** Novelty, creative AI use
2. **Strategic Quality (25 points):** Strategy depth, ROI accuracy, governance quality
3. **Business Value (25 points):** Organizational impact, investment value, strategic alignment
4. **Presentation (25 points):** Clear demo, effective communication, executive readiness

**Total:** 100 points
