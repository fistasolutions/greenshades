# Finance Department: Pre-Hackathon Milestones

**Department:** Finance  
**Hackathon:** AI Financial Analysis & Automation  
**Duration:** 8 Weeks  
**Target Audience:** Finance Managers, Financial Analysts, Accountants

---

## Overview

This 8-week pre-hackathon program prepares Finance teams for the AI Agentic AI Hackathon by building foundational knowledge in AI-powered financial analysis, reporting automation, and compliance.

---

### Learning Objectives
- Review hackathon problem statements
- Form teams and assign roles
- Prepare financial tools
- Final skill validation

**Week 7: Problem Statement Review**
- [ ] Review all 3 Finance hackathon problem statements
- [ ] Research each problem domain

- [ ] Prepare initial financial strategy

**Week 7: Team Formation**
- [ ] Form teams (3-5 people)

- [ ] Schedule team meetings

**Week 8: Tool Preparation**
