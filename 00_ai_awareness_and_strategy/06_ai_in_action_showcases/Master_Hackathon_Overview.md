# Master AI Agentic AI Hackathon Overview

**Title:** Master AI Agentic AI Hackathon Program for Greenshades  
**Audience:** All Departments (Engineering, QA, Product, HR, Finance, Sales, Support, Leadership)  
**Duration:** 8-12 Weeks (Pre-Hackathon) + 2-3 Days (Hackathon)  
**Status:** World-Class Hackathon Program

---

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive hackathon program provides department-specific AI Agentic AI hackathons with structured pre-hackathon milestones. Each department has a tailored hackathon designed to solve real business problems using AI and agentic AI technologies.

**Program Structure:**
- **Pre-Hackathon Phase:** 8-12 weeks of preparation and skill building
- **Hackathon Phase:** 2-3 days of intensive development and innovation
- **Post-Hackathon Phase:** Evaluation, production development, and scaling

## Hackathon Program Overview

### Department Hackathons

| Department | Hackathon Focus | Duration | Teams | Problem Statements |
|------------|----------------|----------|-------|---------------------|

| **Support** | Chatbot Development & Ticket Routing | 2 days | 3-5 teams | 3 problems |
| **Leadership** | Strategic Planning & ROI Analysis | 1 day | 2-3 teams | 2 problems |

### Program Timeline

**Phase 1: Pre-Hackathon Preparation (Weeks 1-8)**
- Week 1-2: AI Fundamentals Training
- Week 3-4: Tool Proficiency Development
- Week 5-6: Department-Specific Skills
- Week 7-8: Problem Statement Preparation

**Phase 2: Hackathon Execution (Days 1-3)**
- Day 1: Kickoff, Team Formation, Development Start
- Day 2: Development, Mentoring, Check-ins
- Day 3: Final Development, Presentations, Judging, Awards

**Phase 3: Post-Hackathon (Weeks 9-12)**
- Week 9: Evaluation and Selection
- Week 10-12: Production Development (selected projects)

## Pre-Hackathon Milestones Framework

### Milestone Structure (All Departments)

**Milestone 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)**
- Complete AI fundamentals training
- Understand agentic AI concepts
- Learn MCP basics
- Tool setup and configuration

**Milestone 2: Tool Proficiency (Weeks 3-4)**
- Master department-specific AI tools
- Complete hands-on exercises
- Build first prototypes
- Achieve tool proficiency

**Milestone 3: Department Skills (Weeks 5-6)**
- Learn department-specific use cases
- Study real-world examples
- Practice with department scenarios
- Build confidence

**Milestone 4: Hackathon Readiness (Weeks 7-8)**
- Review problem statements
- Form teams
- Prepare development environment
- Final skill assessments

## Hackathon Structure (All Departments)

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM):**

- Problem statement presentation
- Team formation
- Tool access and setup
- Q&A session

**Afternoon (1 PM - 6 PM):**
- Team planning and design
- Development begins
- Mentor check-ins (2 PM, 4 PM)
- Progress tracking

**Evening (Optional):**
- Continued development
- Team collaboration
- Mentor availability

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM):**
- Development continues
- Mid-day check-in (10:30 AM)
- Mentor support
- Progress review

**Afternoon (1 PM - 6 PM):**
- Final development
- Testing and refinement
- Presentation preparation
- Final mentor check-in (4 PM)

**Evening:**
- Final touches
- Presentation practice

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM):**
- Project presentations (10-15 min each)
- Demo and Q&A
- Judging

**Afternoon (1 PM - 4 PM):**
- Final judging and deliberation
- Awards ceremony
- Celebration and networking

## Judging Criteria (All Departments)

### Standard Criteria (100 points total)

1. **Innovation (25 points)**
   - Novelty of approach
   - Creative problem-solving
   - Use of AI capabilities

2. **Technical Quality (25 points)**
   - Code/implementation quality
   - Architecture and design
   - Tool usage and integration

3. **Business Value (25 points)**
   - Problem solved
   - Impact and ROI potential
   - Production readiness

4. **Presentation (25 points)**
   - Clear communication
   - Effective demo
   - Compelling story

### Department-Specific Additions

- **Engineering:** Code quality, test coverage, documentation
- **QA:** Test coverage, automation level, quality metrics
- **Product:** PRD quality, strategic alignment, user value
- **HR:** Process improvement, employee impact, scalability
- **Finance:** Accuracy, compliance, cost savings
- **Sales:** Lead quality, conversion potential, automation
- **Support:** Resolution rate, user satisfaction, scalability
- **Leadership:** Strategic value, ROI, organizational impact
