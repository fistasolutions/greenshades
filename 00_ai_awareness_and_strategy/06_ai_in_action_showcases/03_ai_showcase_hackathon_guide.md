## Core Content

### What is an AI Showcase Hackathon?

**AI Showcase Hackathon** is a time-boxed event (1-3 days) where teams build AI-powered solutions to real business problems, showcase their work, and learn from each other.

**Goals:**
- **Innovation:** Explore new AI use cases and solutions
- **Learning:** Hands-on experience with AI tools and techniques
- **Collaboration:** Cross-functional teams working together
- **Showcase:** Demonstrate AI capabilities and value

**Benefits:**
- Rapid prototyping of AI ideas
- Team building and collaboration
- Knowledge sharing and learning
- Identification of production-ready solutions

---

### Hackathon Planning

#### Phase 1: Preparation (2-4 weeks before)

**Define Goals:**
- What problems are we solving?
- What AI capabilities are we exploring?
- What outcomes do we want?

**Example Goals:**
- Build 5 AI prototypes for payroll/tax use cases
- Explore agentic AI capabilities
- Identify 2-3 production-ready solutions
- Train 50+ team members on AI tools

**Set Success Criteria:**
- Number of projects completed
- Quality of solutions
- Team participation
- Production-ready candidates

---

#### Phase 2: Logistics (1-2 weeks before)

**Venue and Timing:**
- **Duration:** 1-3 days (recommend 2 days)
- **Location:** On-site or virtual (or hybrid)
- **Schedule:** Clear agenda with breaks

**Resources:**
- AI tools access (Copilot, Cursor, ChatGPT, Claude)
- Development environments
- Sample data (anonymized)
- Mentors and experts
- Prizes and recognition

**Teams:**
- **Size:** 3-5 people per team
- **Composition:** Mix of roles (dev, QA, product, etc.)
- **Formation:** Self-organize or assigned

---

#### Phase 3: Execution (Hackathon Days)

**Day 1: Kickoff and Development**
- **Morning:** Kickoff, problem statements, team formation
- **Afternoon:** Development and prototyping
- **Evening:** Progress check-ins, mentoring

**Day 2: Development and Showcase**
- **Morning:** Final development and testing
- **Afternoon:** Presentations and judging
- **Evening:** Awards and celebration

---

### Problem Statements

#### Problem 1: Payroll Anomaly Detection
**Challenge:** Detect payroll anomalies automatically  
**Tools:** AI analysis, MCP integration  
**Success:** Flag anomalies with 90%+ accuracy  
**Impact:** 90% time savings, faster error detection

#### Problem 2: Tax Code Classification
**Challenge:** Automatically classify transactions into tax codes  
**Tools:** NLP, classification models  
**Success:** 95%+ classification accuracy  
**Impact:** 85% time savings, improved accuracy

#### Problem 3: Support Ticket Routing
**Challenge:** Automatically route support tickets to right team  
**Tools:** NLP, classification, MCP integration  
**Success:** 80%+ routing accuracy  
**Impact:** 50% faster resolution, better satisfaction

#### Problem 4: Test Case Generation
**Challenge:** Generate test cases from product requirements  
**Tools:** Spec-driven development, AI test generators  
**Success:** 70% faster test creation  
**Impact:** Improved coverage, faster delivery

#### Problem 5: Document Processing
**Challenge:** Extract data from W-2s and tax forms  
**Tools:** Computer vision, NLP, OCR  
**Success:** 90%+ extraction accuracy  
**Impact:** 80% time savings, reduced errors

---

### Hackathon Structure

#### Kickoff (30 minutes)
- Welcome and goals
- Problem statements
- Team formation
- Tool access and setup
- Q&A

#### Development (Day 1: 6-8 hours)
- Team planning and design
- Development and prototyping
- Testing and iteration
- Mentor check-ins

#### Showcase (Day 2: 2-3 hours)
- Project presentations (5-10 min each)
- Demo and Q&A
- Judging and evaluation
- Awards and recognition

---

### Judging Criteria

#### Innovation (25%)
- Novelty of approach
- Creative problem-solving
- Use of AI capabilities

#### Technical Quality (25%)
- Code quality and architecture
- AI model accuracy and performance
- Integration and tool usage

#### Business Value (25%)
- Problem solved
- Impact and ROI potential
- Production readiness

#### Presentation (25%)
- Clear communication
- Effective demo
- Compelling story

**Scoring:** 1-5 scale for each criterion (total: 20 points)

---

### Awards and Recognition

**Categories:**
- **Best Overall:** Highest total score
- **Most Innovative:** Best use of AI capabilities
- **Best Business Value:** Highest impact potential
- **Best Technical:** Highest code quality
- **People's Choice:** Audience vote

**Prizes:**
- Recognition and awards
- Presentation to leadership
- Opportunity to develop for production
- Learning and development credits

---

### Post-Hackathon

#### Evaluation
- **Review Projects:** Evaluate production readiness
- **Identify Winners:** Select projects for development
- **Gather Feedback:** Survey participants
- **Document Learnings:** Capture best practices

#### Production Development
- **Select Projects:** Choose 2-3 for production
- **Allocate Resources:** Assign teams and budget
- **Develop Roadmap:** Plan development timeline
- **Measure Success:** Track progress and impact

- "Prepare problem statements that are challenging but achievable."
