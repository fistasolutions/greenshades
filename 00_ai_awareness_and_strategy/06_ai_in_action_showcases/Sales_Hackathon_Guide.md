# Sales Department: AI Lead Qualification Hackathon

**Department:** Sales  
**Duration:** 2 Days  
**Teams:** 3-5 teams (3-5 people each)

---

## Hackathon Overview

### Objective
Build innovative AI-powered sales solutions that automate lead qualification, enhance proposal generation, and improve sales productivity.

### Theme
"10√ó Sales Productivity: AI Lead Qualification, Proposal Generation, and CRM Automation"

---

## Problem Statements

### Problem 1: AI Lead Qualification Agent
**Challenge:** Create an AI agent that automatically qualifies leads, scores them, and routes to appropriate sales representatives.

**Requirements:**
- Qualify leads automatically
- Score leads by fit and potential
- Route leads to sales reps
- Generate lead summaries
- Support multiple lead sources

**Expected Impact:**
- 70% reduction in qualification time
- 90% accuracy in lead scoring
- Faster lead response

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (30%), Technical Quality (30%), Business Value (20%), Presentation (20%)

---

### Problem 2: Proposal Generation System
**Challenge:** Build an AI system that automatically generates personalized sales proposals based on client requirements.

**Requirements:**
- Generate proposals automatically
- Customize for client needs
- Include pricing and terms
- Create proposal templates
- Support multiple proposal types

**Expected Impact:**
- 60% reduction in proposal creation time
- Consistent proposal quality
- Faster client response

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (25%), Technical Quality (35%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)

---

### Problem 3: CRM Automation Agent
**Challenge:** Create an AI agent that automates CRM data entry, updates records, and generates sales reports.

**Requirements:**
- Automate CRM data entry
- Update records automatically
- Generate sales reports
- Track customer interactions
- Support multiple CRM systems

**Expected Impact:**
- 80% reduction in data entry time
- Accurate CRM data
- Better sales insights

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (20%), Technical Quality (40%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)

---

## Judging Criteria

1. **Innovation (25 points):** Novelty, creative AI use
2. **Technical Quality (25 points):** Automation quality, accuracy, integration
3. **Business Value (25 points):** Sales impact, time savings, conversion improvement
4. **Presentation (25 points):** Clear demo, effective communication

**Total:** 100 points
