# Product Department: Pre-Hackathon Milestones

**Department:** Product  
**Hackathon:** AI PRD Generation & Product Strategy  
**Duration:** 8 Weeks  
**Target Audience:** Product Managers, Product Owners, Product Strategists

---

## Overview

This 8-week pre-hackathon program prepares Product teams for the AI Agentic AI Hackathon by building foundational knowledge in AI-powered PRD generation, product strategy, and ChatPRD usage.

---

## Milestone 1: AI Fundamentals (Weeks 1-2)

### Learning Objectives
- Understand AI and agentic AI concepts
- Learn AI applications in product management
- Understand PRD generation with AI
- Complete foundational training

### Required Curriculum Modules
- ✅ `00_introduction_to_ai_and_agentic_ai/` (all 6 lessons)
- ✅ `01_ai_vs_ai_assisted_vs_ai_driven/` (all 4 lessons)
- ✅ `04_chatprd_generation/readme.md` (complete guide)
- ✅ `05_productivity_10x_framework/02_role_based_productivity_examples.md` (Product section)

### Hands-On Exercises

**Exercise 1: ChatPRD Setup**
- [ ] Create ChatPRD account
- [ ] Explore ChatPRD interface
- [ ] Test basic PRD generation
- [ ] Review PRD templates

**Exercise 2: First AI-Generated PRD**
- [ ] Generate PRD for simple feature
- [ ] Generate user stories
- [ ] Generate technical specifications
- [ ] Compare AI vs manual PRD quality

**Exercise 3: Understanding Product AI**
- [ ] Review product management AI use cases
- [ ] Understand PRD generation workflows
- [ ] Study product strategy with AI
- [ ] Identify AI opportunities

### Assessment Checkpoint
- [ ] Complete all curriculum modules
- [ ] Successfully generate PRD with ChatPRD
- [ ] Understand AI product management concepts
- [ ] Can explain PRD generation workflows

**Readiness Criteria:** ✅ All exercises completed, all assessments passed

## Milestone 4: Hackathon Readiness (Weeks 7-8)

### Learning Objectives
- Review hackathon problem statements
- Form teams and assign roles
- Prepare product tools
- Final skill validation

### Required Activities

**Week 7: Problem Statement Review**
- [ ] Review all 4 Product hackathon problem statements
- [ ] Research each problem domain
- [ ] Identify preferred problem to solve
- [ ] Prepare initial product strategy

**Week 7: Team Formation**
- [ ] Form teams (3-5 people)
- [ ] Assign roles (product lead, strategist, analyst)
- [ ] Set team communication
- [ ] Schedule team meetings

**Week 8: Tool Preparation**
- [ ] Set up ChatPRD and AI tools
- [ ] Prepare product templates
- [ ] Gather product data
- [ ] Test tool integrations

**Week 8: Final Validation**
- [ ] Complete mock hackathon exercise
- [ ] Generate PRD for sample feature
- [ ] Present to team for feedback
- [ ] Refine approach

### Assessment Checkpoint
- [ ] Team formed and roles assigned
- [ ] Tools prepared
- [ ] Problem statement selected
- [ ] Strategy prepared
- [ ] Mock exercise completed

**Readiness Criteria:** ✅ All preparations complete, ready for hackathon
