# Product Department: AI PRD Generation Hackathon

**Department:** Product  
**Duration:** 2 Days  
**Teams:** 4-6 teams (3-5 people each)

---

## Hackathon Overview

### Objective
Build innovative AI-powered product management solutions that accelerate PRD generation, improve product strategy, and enhance product decision-making.

### Theme
"10√ó Product Productivity: AI PRD Generation, Strategy, and Product Analytics"

---

## Problem Statements

### Problem 1: AI PRD Generator
**Challenge:** Create an AI agent that automatically generates comprehensive PRDs from product ideas, including user stories, technical specs, and acceptance criteria.

**Requirements:**
- Generate PRDs from product ideas
- Include user stories and technical specs
- Create acceptance criteria
- Generate product documentation
- Support multiple product types

**Expected Impact:**
- 70% reduction in PRD creation time
- Consistent PRD quality
- Faster product planning

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (30%), Technical Quality (30%), Business Value (20%), Presentation (20%)

---

### Problem 2: Product Strategy Generator
**Challenge:** Build an AI system that generates product strategies, roadmaps, and go-to-market plans from market analysis.

**Requirements:**
- Generate product strategies
- Create product roadmaps
- Develop go-to-market plans
- Include market analysis
- Generate competitive analysis

**Expected Impact:**
- 60% faster strategy development
- Better strategic alignment
- Improved market positioning

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (25%), Technical Quality (35%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)

---

### Problem 3: Product Analytics Agent
**Challenge:** Create an AI agent that analyzes product data, generates insights, and provides product recommendations.

**Requirements:**
- Analyze product usage data
- Generate insights and recommendations
- Create product reports
- Predict product trends
- Provide actionable recommendations

**Expected Impact:**
- 80% reduction in analysis time
- Better product decisions
- Data-driven product strategy

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (20%), Technical Quality (40%), Business Value (25%), Presentation (15%)

---

### Problem 4: Feature Prioritization System
**Challenge:** Build an AI system that prioritizes features based on user value, business impact, and technical feasibility.

**Requirements:**
- Analyze feature requests
- Prioritize based on multiple criteria
- Generate prioritization reports
- Support decision-making
- Integrate with product roadmaps

**Expected Impact:**
- 50% faster prioritization
- Better feature selection
- Improved product ROI

**Judging Weight:** Innovation (25%), Technical Quality (35%), Business Value (20%), Presentation (20%)

---

## Judging Criteria

1. **Innovation (25 points):** Novelty, creative AI use
2. **Technical Quality (25 points):** PRD quality, strategy depth, analytics accuracy
3. **Business Value (25 points):** Product impact, strategic value, ROI
4. **Presentation (25 points):** Clear demo, effective communication

**Total:** 100 points
