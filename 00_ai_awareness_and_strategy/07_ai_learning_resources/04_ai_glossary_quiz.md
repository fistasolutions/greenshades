### Section 1: AI Fundamentals (10 questions)

#### Question 1
What does "AI" stand for?
a) Automated Intelligence
b) Artificial Intelligence
c) Advanced Intelligence
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Artificial Intelligence

---

#### Question 2
True or False: All AI systems today are Narrow AI (ANI), specialized for specific tasks.

**Answer:** True

---

#### Question 3
What is the difference between AI and traditional software?
a) AI follows explicit rules, traditional software learns from data
b) AI learns from data, traditional software follows explicit rules
c) There is no difference
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) AI learns from data, traditional software follows explicit rules

---

#### Question 4
What type of learning uses labeled examples with correct answers?
a) Supervised Learning
b) Unsupervised Learning
c) Reinforcement Learning
d) None of the above

**Answer:** a) Supervised Learning

---

#### Question 5
What is "hallucination" in AI?
a) AI being creative
b) AI generating false information confidently
c) AI being confused
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) AI generating false information confidently

---

#### Question 6
What does "LLM" stand for?
a) Large Learning Model
b) Large Language Model
c) Long Language Model
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Large Language Model

---

#### Question 7
True or False: AI can always be trusted to produce accurate results without verification.

**Answer:** False

---

#### Question 8
What is "fine-tuning" in AI?
a) Making AI models smaller
b) Adapting general models for specific tasks
c) Making AI models faster
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Adapting general models for specific tasks

---

#### Question 9
What is a "token" in language models?
a) A security key
b) Basic unit of text processed by language models
c) A type of AI model
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Basic unit of text processed by language models

---

#### Question 10
What is "prompt engineering"?
a) Building AI models
b) Crafting effective prompts to get desired AI outputs
c) Testing AI models
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Crafting effective prompts to get desired AI outputs

---

### Section 2: AI Development Paradigms (10 questions)

#### Question 11
What is the key difference between AI-assisted and AI-driven development?
a) AI-assisted uses better tools
b) AI-driven generates code from specifications, AI-assisted suggests completions
c) There is no difference
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) AI-driven generates code from specifications, AI-assisted suggests completions

---

#### Question 12
True or False: In AI-assisted development, the developer remains in control and makes final decisions.

**Answer:** True

---

#### Question 13
What is "Spec-Driven Development"?
a) Writing code first, then specifications
b) Writing specifications first, then AI generates code
c) Writing tests first
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Writing specifications first, then AI generates code

---

#### Question 14
What does "MCP" stand for?
a) Model Communication Protocol
b) Model Context Protocol
c) Machine Context Protocol
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Model Context Protocol

---

#### Question 15
What is "Agentic AI"?
a) AI that only answers questions
b) AI that plans and executes autonomous workflows
c) AI that only generates code
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) AI that plans and executes autonomous workflows

---

#### Question 16
True or False: Agentic AI can use tools like databases, APIs, and file systems through MCP.

**Answer:** True

---

#### Question 17
What SpecKit command generates code from a specification?
a) `/specify`
b) `/implement`
c) `/testgen`
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) `/implement`

---

#### Question 18
What is "orchestration" in agentic AI?
a) Playing music
b) Coordination of multiple tools and steps in workflows
c) Training AI models
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Coordination of multiple tools and steps in workflows

---

#### Question 19
True or False: AI agents can autonomously plan multi-step workflows without human input.

**Answer:** True (but human oversight recommended for critical decisions)

---

#### Question 20
What is the primary benefit of MCP for AI agents?
a) Faster processing
b) Standardized, secure tool access with orchestration
c) Free tool access
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Standardized, secure tool access with orchestration

---

### Section 3: AI Ethics and Security (10 questions)

#### Question 21
What is "AI bias"?
a) AI being unfair
b) Systematic errors or unfairness in AI outputs
c) AI making mistakes
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Systematic errors or unfairness in AI outputs

---

#### Question 22
True or False: AI systems should be tested for bias across demographic groups.

**Answer:** True

---

#### Question 23
What is "AI auditability"?
a) AI being audited by accountants
b) Ability to trace, review, and verify AI decisions
c) AI checking its own work
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Ability to trace, review, and verify AI decisions

---

#### Question 24
What should you never paste into external AI tools?
a) Code
b) Sensitive data (SSNs, salaries, passwords)
c) Questions
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Sensitive data (SSNs, salaries, passwords)

---

#### Question 25
True or False: Human oversight is required for critical AI decisions like payroll and tax calculations.

**Answer:** True

---

#### Question 26
What is a responsible AI use principle?
a) Use AI for everything
b) Fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, safety, human oversight
c) Only use AI for simple tasks
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, safety, human oversight

---

#### Question 27
What should you use instead of real sensitive data in AI tools?
a) Nothing
b) Placeholders like `<EXAMPLE_ONLY>`
c) Fake data that looks real
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Placeholders like `<EXAMPLE_ONLY>`

---

#### Question 28
True or False: All AI actions must be auditable for compliance.

**Answer:** True

---

#### Question 29
What is "transparency" in AI?
a) AI being see-through
b) Ability to understand how AI makes decisions
c) AI being fast
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Ability to understand how AI makes decisions

---

#### Question 30
What should you do if you accidentally paste sensitive data into an AI tool?
a) Ignore it
b) Report it immediately to IT/Security
c) Delete it yourself
d) None of the above

**Answer:** b) Report it immediately to IT/Security
