## Core Principles

1. **Security First:** Never compromise security for productivity
2. **Data Privacy:** Protect sensitive data (SSNs, salaries, passwords)
3. **Quality Assurance:** Always review and verify AI outputs
4. **Responsible Use:** Use AI ethically and responsibly
5. **Compliance:** Follow all regulations (GDPR, CCPA, payroll laws)

## Data Security Rules

### Never Paste
- ❌ Employee SSNs, salaries, personal information
- ❌ Customer financial data
- ❌ Passwords, API keys, credentials
- ❌ Proprietary business information

### Use Placeholders
- ✅ `<EXAMPLE_ONLY>` for sensitive data
- ✅ `<EMPLOYEE_ID>` for employee identifiers
- ✅ `<SALARY_AMOUNT>` for salary data
- ✅ `<CUSTOMER_NAME>` for customer data

---

## Code Security

### Review Requirements
- ✅ Review all AI-generated code before using
- ✅ Check for security vulnerabilities (SQL injection, XSS, etc.)
- ✅ Verify business logic (especially payroll/tax calculations)
- ✅ Test thoroughly before production deployment

### Prohibited
- ❌ Hardcoded credentials
- ❌ Security vulnerabilities
- ❌ Bypassing security controls
- ❌ Violating compliance rules

---

## Quality Assurance

### Verification
- ✅ Test all AI-generated code
- ✅ Verify business logic manually
- ✅ Check edge cases and error handling
- ✅ Review documentation for accuracy

### Payroll/Tax Specific
- ✅ Always verify calculations manually
- ✅ Check tax rates and regulations
- ✅ Ensure compliance with payroll laws
- ✅ Audit all AI decisions affecting payroll

---

## Responsible Use

### Ethical Guidelines
- ✅ Use AI to enhance, not replace, human judgment
- ✅ Be transparent about AI usage
- ✅ Avoid bias and discrimination
- ✅ Respect privacy and confidentiality

### Prohibited Uses
- ❌ Using AI to bypass security or compliance
- ❌ Using AI for discriminatory purposes
- ❌ Using AI to generate misleading information
- ❌ Using AI without proper review and verification

---

## Compliance

### Regulations
- ✅ GDPR (data privacy)
- ✅ CCPA (California privacy)
- ✅ Payroll and tax regulations
- ✅ Industry-specific compliance

### Audit Requirements
- ✅ All AI actions must be auditable
- ✅ Maintain logs of AI usage
- ✅ Document AI decisions affecting business
- ✅ Report security incidents immediately

---

## Violations

### Consequences
- Violations may result in:
  - Revocation of AI tool access
  - Disciplinary action
  - Legal consequences (if regulations violated)

### Reporting
- Report violations to IT/Security immediately
- Report security concerns immediately
- Report compliance issues immediately
