## Essential AI Terms

### Foundation Terms
- **AI (Artificial Intelligence):** Machines performing human-like tasks
- **LLM (Large Language Model):** AI model trained on massive text (GPT, Claude)
- **Agentic AI:** AI that plans and executes autonomous workflows
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol):** Standard protocol for agent-tool communication
- **Narrow AI (ANI):** AI specialized for specific tasks (current reality)
- **General AI (AGI):** Human-level intelligence (future goal)

### Development Terms
- **AI-Assisted Development:** Developer uses AI tools, remains in control
- **AI-Driven Development:** AI generates code from specifications
- **Spec-Driven Development:** Write specs, AI implements
- **Prompt Engineering:** Crafting effective AI instructions
- **Fine-Tuning:** Adapting general models for specific domains

### Learning Types
- **Supervised Learning:** Learning from labeled examples
- **Unsupervised Learning:** Discovering patterns in unlabeled data
- **Reinforcement Learning:** Learning through rewards/penalties

### Technical Terms
- **Transformer:** Neural network architecture (enables LLMs)
- **Token:** Basic unit of text processed by language models
- **Context Window:** Maximum text an AI can process at once
- **Embedding:** Numerical representation of text/data
- **Hallucination:** AI generating false information confidently
- **Bias:** Unfair or incorrect AI assumptions

### Process Terms
- **Training:** Teaching AI with examples
- **Inference:** Using trained AI on new data
- **Prompt:** Input instruction to AI model
- **Specification:** Description of what code should do
