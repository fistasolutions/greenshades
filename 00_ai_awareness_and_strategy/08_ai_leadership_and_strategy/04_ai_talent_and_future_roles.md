## Core Content

### How AI is Changing Roles

**AI Augments, Not Replaces:**
- AI handles repetitive, rule-based tasks
- Humans focus on strategic, creative, complex work
- Roles evolve, new skills required
- New roles emerge (AI specialists, prompt engineers)

**Role Evolution:**
- **Before AI:** Manual, repetitive tasks
- **With AI:** Strategic, high-value work
- **Future:** AI-enhanced, more productive roles
